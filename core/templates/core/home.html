How the Application Works

A look behind the scenes of our prediction pipeline.

1. Data Input

You provide passenger details such as Name, Age, Sex, Ticket, Siblings, Parents and Port details through our secure form.

2. ML Processing

Our Random Forest model analyzes the input against patterns found in the 891 historical records from the original dataset.

3. Instant Results

The app generates a survival probability score and logs the result in your personal prediction history.
